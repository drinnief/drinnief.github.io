Web Expression


My intention of this website is to contrast the level of customization people used to have on the internet before social media,
and how much people during that time are able to express themselves compared to today.
This project leans onto the idea of personal websites. Which were popular during the 90’s and early Internet.
Though, in the modern day, there has been a resurgence of personal websites.
I decided to lean on both of those aesthetics by using a lot of assets (gifs, images, etc.) shared around in those communities.
