Glitch Art


An artwork I created by changing an image's code. I used screenshots from my iPad, laptop, and phone.
Each image presents different kinds of digital clutter from online spaces.
Although they are not tangible, they still cause mental strain on the user, similar to physical clutter.
The glitching affect used in these images are intended to represent feelings of overwhelmed and over-stimulation.
